Voice Over...and Me


Let me tell a story. Let me tell your story. Let me be the voice that connects the imagery, the music, and the audience to your brand. That’s the magic of voice over. The right voice has the power to quickly inspire and motivate your audience to call your office, to pick your product off the shelf vs the competitors, to drive an extra 20 minutes just to reach the company they heard me talk about. Their interest has been piqued. The rest is up to you. Let me be your voice.


Houston - Austin - Los Angeles. My journey has taken me many places, but I am sixteen years strong here in southern California and so far, no one has kicked me out. Although most of my industry experience has been in front of the camera, voice over has been the art form that has resonated with me the most. My voice translates across a myriad of industries from healthcare, hospitality, non-profit, creative, corporate, and more…I believe in professionalism and prompt and thorough service for my clients. Let’s make voice over magic! Thank you in advance.
